Instructions

"a" = Left

"s" = Down

"d" = Right

Spacebar = Jump

"j" = Shoot

"k" = Dash

Description:

Kill the Big Bad Wolf as Miss Piggy! Dodge knives, forks, and other attacks from the Wolf's 3 phases while shooting fireballs from your pure rage! Four lives are given and dashing is not invincible.

If the platforms are close to each other and the game is running slow/poorly, ensure that no applications are running in the background. Adjust your browser's zoom percentage if you cannot view the entire game.
